Welcome to the ToDo App!

The ToDo App is a lightweight, interactive task tracker! Use it to keep track of the things you need to do. Perfect for homework, chores, or just getting to know your colleagues.

How It Works

  • Home Page: This landing page explains what the app is and how to use it. Log in here to access your tasks.
  • Task Page: Complete tasks on any list! Enjoy creative task statuses like complete or incomplete, and track your progress as you check boxes.

Features

  • Store and retrieve your personal tasks
  • Upload and view tasks
  • Responsive design for all devices
